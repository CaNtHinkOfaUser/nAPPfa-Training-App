It is common knowledge that each of us needs to be physically and mentally fit to thrive amidst academic rigour and also participate in and pass a critical physical fitness test every other year - the NAPFA (National Physical Fitness Award) test. Yet the issue we often face, alongside our peers in SST (age 13-16), is the ambition to meet our physical fitness goals, but we are being hampered by a lack of time and motivation for what could work for us. This leads to inconsistency, which is very prevalent in this day and age. 

This app allows students in SST to attain their fitness goals in the NAPFA test gradually. We use a routine system to ensure that students are disciplined to want to achieve and work towards their desired goals. Our app is a tool for students to help them in their journey, by tracking their progress and setting workout plans for them. 
We aim to launch this app nationwide across all secondary schools in singapore. In the future, we can tweak our app for newer audiences such as the working adult population or a tool that allows caregivers to plan out a routine for the elderly to stay active. We believe that our app can help everyone achieve their fitness goals.
